Preheader
WEEK 5 — Smash factor tells you if you're making clean contact
Body

Headline: Smash factor: the quality of your strike

Body copy: Smash factor is ball speed divided by club head speed. It tells you how cleanly you're striking the ball. A 1.50 smash factor with a driver means you're transferring energy efficiently. Below 1.40? You're leaving 10-20 yards on the table with off-center hits. Check your smash factor in your session data this week.

Benchmarks:

  • Driver: 1.44-1.50 (tour avg 1.49)
  • 7-iron: 1.33-1.36
  • Wedges: 1.22-1.25

Journey Canvas Build

Step-by-step
1

Create the Journey

  1. Navigate to Messaging → Journeys → New Journey
  2. Journey Name: "Weekly Practice Habit Builder"
  3. Description: "8-week progressive habit formation journey for active paid subscribers. 24 touchpoints across email, push, and in-app."
  4. Folder: Journey Builder (1340586)
2

Configure Entry Trigger

  1. Entry source: List Membership
  2. List: "Active Paid — 1+ session in 14 days"
  3. Re-entry: Allow re-entry after journey completion (users who complete can re-enter on next list refresh)
  4. Filter: NOT currently in journey "At-Risk Recovery"
3

Configure Exit Conditions

  1. Click Journey Settings → Exit Conditions
  2. Add exit condition 1:
    Condition: User field mlm2_days_since_last_session >= 14
    Action: Move to journey "At-Risk Recovery"
  3. Add exit condition 2:
    Condition: Custom event customer.subscription.deleted fired
    Action: Exit journey immediately
4
